About GLEAMS

GLEAMS (the Graduate Linguistics Expo at Michigan State) is Michigan State University's Department of Linguistics and Languages' departmental graduate student colloquium, showcasing graduate work in phonology, syntax, semantics, psycholinguistics, sociolinguistics, and child language acquisition. This conference gives students the opportunity to present their work to colleagues within and outside the department.

This year's GLEAMS will take place February 27th and 28th, 2016.

Invited Speakers

This year's invited speakers are Donca Steriade (MIT) and Richard Larson (Stony Brook).
